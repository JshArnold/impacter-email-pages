How it works
STEP 01
Students respond in their own voice.
No checkboxes. No Likert scales. A thoughtful prompt appears — students respond by speaking. VideoAsk captures authentic, unscripted responses that reveal far more than any survey question.
STEP 02
AI scores against behavioral health rubrics.
Each response is transcribed and scored against three behavioral health domains using IMPACTER's proprietary rubric. Scores surface within minutes — not weeks.
STEP 03
District leaders see the full picture.
The Percy dashboard gives coordinators cohort-level insights, individual student profiles, and trend data — built for MTSS identification and early intervention.
STEP 04
Integrated into your MTSS framework.
Screener data maps directly onto MTSS tiers. Universal screening at Tier 1, targeted student lists for Tier 2 check-ins, and individual profiles that inform Tier 3 supports.
