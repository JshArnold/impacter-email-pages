Scoring Framework / Behavioral Health Engagement Rubric
Level Name Description MTSS Tier
4Extending Student demonstrates proactive BH awareness, seeks growth opportunities, and can articulate coping strategies with specificity and agency. Tier 1
3Meeting Student shows consistent self-awareness, articulates emotional states, and demonstrates emerging help-seeking behavior with some support. Tier 1
2Approaching Student shows partial BH awareness with limited coping vocabulary. Identifies stressors but struggles to articulate support needs. Monitor closely. Tier 2
1Beginning Student shows minimal BH self-awareness. Avoidant language, flat affect markers, or acute distress signals detected. Immediate counselor review recommended. Tier 3
