MTSS Integration

Screening that maps to your existing support structure

The Behavioral Health Screener doesn't create a new system — it makes your MTSS tiers more precise, more equitable, and faster to activate.

T1
Tier 1 — Universal Screening
All students, all grade bands, 3-5 prompts each
Every student completes voice-based prompts at the start of each semester. AI scoring identifies baseline patterns across all five behavioral health domains at the population level.
T2
Tier 2 — Targeted Support
Students flagged by signal patterns, counselor review
Students with elevated concern scores are surfaced automatically to counseling teams with domain-level evidence — not just a number, but the actual language that triggered the flag.
T3
Tier 3 — Intensive Intervention
High-need students, case coordination, documentation
For students with persistent or acute signals, screener data feeds directly into case documentation and helps clinicians track growth across re-screening intervals.

Scoring System

Every response scored 1–4 against a validated engagement rubric.

Total score out of 24 across six behavioral health domains. MTSS tier assignment is automatic — no manual review required.

1
Minimal
Surface-level or one-word response. Limited engagement with the prompt.
2
Basic
Some elaboration, limited personal reflection. Beginning to engage.
3
Developing
Clear reflection, some emotional vocabulary. Growth orientation emerging.
4
Extended
Nuanced, emotionally specific, growth-oriented thinking. High signal.

State Validation · BHSSA-002

Selected for California's statewide behavioral health evaluation.

In partnership with the Santa Clara County Office of Education, IMPACTER Pathway was selected by the California Behavioral Health Services Oversight and Accountability Commission (BHSOAC) for the statewide behavioral health performance management evaluation.

BHSSA-002 — Statewide evaluation of student behavioral health screening and outcomes. IMPACTER Pathway is one of a small number of district-facing tools selected for this validation study, which will inform California's behavioral health infrastructure for the next decade.

CYBHI Reimbursement

Districts can run this program at no net cost.

Through California's Children and Youth Behavioral Health Initiative (CYBHI), schools can submit for reimbursement on each behavioral health screening administered — effectively offsetting the cost of the program entirely.

$4.81
Reimbursement per administration under CPT 96127
$0

Our team handles the documentation support to help districts configure their billing workflow. For most districts we work with, the screener pays for itself within the first screening window. We can walk through your district's specific reimbursement scenario on a call.
